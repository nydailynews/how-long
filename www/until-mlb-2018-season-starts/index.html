Now that that the waking nightmare of watching either the Eagles or Patriots win Super Bowl LII is done, us New York City sports fans can focus on baseball season. Yes, that baseball season, the place where our hopes are raised, and then lowered, and then raised, and then probably lowered again.

On March 29, all 30 Major League Baseball teams begin a quest for the 115th Fall Classic, and of those 30 teams, the Mets start first. The Mets open up at home, 1:10 p.m., against the Cardinals while the Yankees will be in Toronto (game time 3:37 p.m.) battling the Blue Jays.

Yes, winter will end, yes, the snow will melt, and yes, if we keep counting the seconds until baseball is back we’re that much closer to baseball being back. Follow our count-down clock as it ticks the seconds until the baseball season starts.
