Royal Marriage FAQ

When was the most-recent royal wedding?

The royal wedding was May 19, 2018.

When was the royal wedding before that?

Prince William and Kate Middleton have been married for 7 years. They were married on April 29, 2011 at Westminster Abbey in London.

Anyone else?

Prince Charles and Lady Diana Spencer were married in a lavish, televised ceremony on July 29, 1981. They divorced in 1996 and she was killed in a car crash in Paris one year later.

How long has Queen Elizabeth been married to Prince Philip?

The Queen of the United Kingdom, Elizabeth II, has been married to Prince Philip, Duke of Edinburgh, for 71 years. Their wedding was November 20, 1947.

Who is Meghan Markle?

Meghan Markle is a bi-racial 36-year-old actress born and raised in Los Angeles. She graduated from Northwestern University in 2003.

Who is Prince Harry?

Prince Harry Charles Albert David is the younger son of Prince Charles and Princess Diana.
