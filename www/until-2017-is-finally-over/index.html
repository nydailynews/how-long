This year is on its last legs. MMXVII, or 2017 as us non-Romans call it, is a year many would rather have fast-forwarded through. But we're at the end, so, how about a replay? Glad you asked.

2017 in review: Donald Trump started his new job, Hurricane Harvey hit Houston and Hurricane Maria crippled Puerto Rico. Certain men (finally) started paying the price for sexually harassing women. Nazis made more headlines than Nazis had in any other year of the last half-century. We witnessed another worst mass shooting in modern history. Oh, and don't forget, the Mets disappointed everyone, again.

If it's any indication of the type of year it's been (it is), Google searches for "hellscape" are trending up, up up. The good news? It's almost over. Help us count down to the moment when we can finally put 2017 in the rearview mirror for what can only be a better year ahead.
